Q = Question block with item
q = Question block with coin
B = Bricks with coin
b = Bricks
> = Horizontal pipe piece (always 2 above eachother)
+ = Pipe connector (horizontal to vertical)
v = Vertical pipe piece (always 2 next to eachother)
G = Ground
X = X block
1, 2, 3 etc. = Determined pipe tops (both vertical and horizontal, always 2 next to eachother)
P = Pipe tops (both vertical and horizontal, always 2 next to eachother)
g = Goomba
K = Red koopa
k = Green koopa


Levels by default are 14 tiles high (14 lines long)
